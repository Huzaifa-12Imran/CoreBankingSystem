============================================================
SOFTWARE REQUIREMENTS SPECIFICATION (SRS)
PROJECT: CORE BANKING SYSTEM (CBS)
============================================================

1. PROJECT OVERVIEW
The Core Banking System (CBS) is a robust, centralized database application 
designed to manage a retail bank's day-to-day operations. It ensures data 
integrity, security, and automation for customer management, financial 
transactions, and loan lifecycles.

2. FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS

FR1: Customer Management (CRUD)
- The system shall allow administrators to enroll new customers.
- The system shall support updating existing customer profiles.
- The system shall prevent the deletion of customers with active bank accounts.

FR2: Account and Interest Operations
- The system shall support multiple account types (Savings, Current, Fixed Deposit).
- The system shall auto-generate unique IBAN-formatted account numbers.
- The system shall track and log daily/monthly Interest Accruals as a dedicated entity.

FR3: Transaction Processing
- The system shall facilitate Deposits, Withdrawals, and Internal Transfers.
- All transactions must follow ACID properties for data consistency.
- The system shall block withdrawals that exceed the available balance.

FR4: Loan and Collateral Management
- The system shall calculate EMIs and generate repayment schedules.
- The system shall manage Collateral (Property, Vehicles, Gold) linked to specific loans.
- The system shall update outstanding balances automatically upon payment.

FR5: System Auditing
- The system shall maintain a "Live Audit Trail" capturing every database change.
- Logs must include operation type, timestamp, and the performing user.

3. SYSTEM AND NON-FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS
- DBMS: PostgreSQL (Relational).
- Normalization: 3rd Normal Form (3NF).
- Security: DCL-based user roles (Admin, Teller, Manager).
- Performance: B-Tree Indexing for high-speed retrieval.

4. ENTITY LIST
- Account, Customer, Transaction, Branch, Loan, Collateral, Card, Employee, AuditLog, Interest.
